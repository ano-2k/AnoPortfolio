Week 9 - Emotional Intelligence

Session Details

Resource Person: Gowrisha Karuneswaran

Designation: Lecturer, Northern Uni

Topic: Emotional Intelligence

Self-Awareness

  • Self-awareness involves recognizing one’s emotions, strengths, and weaknesses, enabling informed decision-making.
  • Regular self-reflection helps identify emotional triggers, improving personal and professional interactions.
  • Understanding personal values aligns actions with goals, fostering authenticity in leadership roles.
  • Awareness of emotional states enhances communication, ensuring clarity and reducing misunderstandings.

Self-Regulation

  • Self-regulation entails managing emotions to maintain professionalism under pressure or conflict.
  • Practicing impulse control prevents reactive decisions, promoting thoughtful responses in challenging situations.
  • Adaptability in regulating emotions ensures resilience when facing unexpected workplace changes.
  • Maintaining accountability for one’s actions builds trust and credibility among team members.

Motivation

  • Intrinsic motivation drives individuals to pursue goals with passion, independent of external rewards.
  • Setting clear, achievable objectives fosters perseverance, even in the face of setbacks.
  • Optimism in approaching challenges inspires others, creating a positive and productive work environment.
  • Aligning personal and organizational goals enhances commitment and sustained effort in tasks.

Empathy

  • Empathy involves understanding others’ emotions and perspectives, fostering stronger interpersonal connections.
  • Active listening to colleagues’ concerns builds trust and supports effective conflict resolution.
  • Recognizing diverse emotional needs promotes inclusivity, enhancing team collaboration and morale.
  • Empathetic leadership adapts communication styles to support team members’ unique challenges.

Social Skills

  • Effective social skills facilitate clear communication, ensuring alignment in team objectives and tasks.
  • Building relationships through networking and collaboration strengthens team cohesion and productivity.
  • Conflict management skills enable constructive resolution, maintaining positive workplace dynamics.
  • Influencing others positively through persuasion and inspiration drives collective success in projects.
