Week 8 - Team and Leadership Skills

Session Details

Resource Person: Gowrisha Karuneswaran

Designation: Lecturer, Northern Uni

Topic: Team and Leadership Skills

Leadership Skills

  • Effective leadership requires articulating a clear vision to align team efforts with organizational objectives.
  • Emotional intelligence enables leaders to understand and manage team dynamics, fostering trust and motivation.
  • Adaptability allows leaders to adjust strategies based on changing circumstances, ensuring resilience in challenging environments.
  • Strategic decision-making involves weighing risks and opportunities, incorporating team input for balanced outcomes.

Teamwork

  • Trust, built through consistent communication and reliability, forms the foundation of effective teamwork.
  • Mutual accountability ensures all members share responsibility for achieving team goals, enhancing cohesion.
  • Clear role definitions prevent overlap and confusion, enabling efficient contributions from each member.
  • Regular feedback fosters continuous improvement, helping teams address challenges and refine processes.

Collaboration

  • Inclusivity in collaboration promotes diverse perspectives, driving innovation and team unity.
  • Effective communication tools and protocols streamline collaborative efforts, minimizing misunderstandings.
  • Proactive conflict management through open dialogue maintains constructive interactions and team harmony.
  • Shared goals create a sense of collective ownership, motivating collaborative success across diverse teams.
